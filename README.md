# Form Filler Chrome Extension

This extension helps you fill out online forms quickly and easily. It saves your data in your browser and uses it to autofill forms on websites.

## How to Use

1.  **Load the extension in Chrome:**
    *   Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions`.
    *   Enable "Developer mode" in the top right corner.
    *   Click "Load unpacked" and select the directory where you saved the files.
2.  **Add your data:**
    *   Click the extension's icon in the Chrome toolbar to open the profile manager.
    *   Add the fields you want to save, like your name, email, phone number, etc.
    *   Click "Save Profile".
3.  **Fill forms:**
    *   When you visit a page with a form, click the extension's icon. The extension will automatically fill in the fields it recognizes.
    *   If the extension finds a field that is not in your profile, it will suggest adding it. Open the popup to add the new field to your profile.
